Tacos Recipe

Decription:

Tacos Al pastor is one of the most storied and famous dishes in all of Mexico

The dish actually has origins and ties to that of lebannon in how they slow roast the pork

The delicious dish is eaten all across Mexico and makes for the perfect meal at just about any point in the day!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Slow cooked pork
  • Slow cooked pork
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa verde

Cooking Insturctions

  1. Put whatever size of pork in a slow cooker for about 4 hours at 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. In the meantime chop up an onion into finely diced pieces
  3. Finely chop Cilantro into very fine segments
  4. Prepare the salsa by taking 3 serrano peppers and 2 gloves of garlic mixed with a pinch or salt and blend until completley smooth
  5. When meat is done cooking fire up the stove on high
  6. Heat tortillas until cooked
  7. Take the cooked pork and chop into fine pieces
  8. Now lay the tortillas on the dish and load them with as much pork as desired
  9. Put desired condiments on tacos
  10. CONGRADULATIONS YOU JUST MADE YOUR FIRST TACOS
